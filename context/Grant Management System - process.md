# Sourcing

## Comprehensive list of initiatives and organizations/individuals with ideas that potentially align with thematic strategy

1. AL, supported by AT, identify a list of initiatives and organizations/individuals with potential connection to the thematic strategy.
2. AL, with AT’s support, enter into a CRM‑type list the initiative, person or organization, point of contact, email, phone number, and a summary of no more than 100 words.
3. AL, with AT’s support, run short scoping calls for initiatives and organizations/individuals with better potential match
4. This work draws on sector expertise, publicly available information, and existing networks.

---

# Screening

## Concept note + institutional pitch/deck shared with ver+

1. For initiatives and organizations/individuals with high strategic fit according to Investment Criteria Set 1, AL requests a concept note (up to 2 pages based on ver+ format) and an institutional deck when available.

## Concept note focused on the rationale for investing (why yes, why not)

1. For initiatives and organizations/individuals with high solution strength and learning value according to Investment Criteria Set 2, AL prepares a concept note (up to 2 pages based on ver+ format) to take to a concept review meeting.

## Concept review meeting minutes

1. AL sends concept note to CEO.
2. CEO selects up to 2 participants for the concept review meeting.
3. AL schedules concept review meeting.

Each initiative should no take more than 30 minutes and more than one initiative can be included in the same concept review meeting.

---

# Investment due diligence

## Complete application form.

1. For initiatives and organizations/individuals approved by the CEO, AL requests a full application form (based on ver+ format).

## Application review report

1. AL informs KMD once the application has been received.
2. KMD process information under protocols.
3. AL and KMD produce report under ver+ format.

## Peer-reviewed memo

1. For initiatives and organizations/individuals with execution capacity and impact/scale potential Investment criteria set 3 AL writes a memo under ver+ format.
2. AL nominates 2 potential peer reviewers.
3. The 2 peer reviewers make comments to the memo.
4. AL answers peer reviewers comments/questions and produce a finalized version of the memo.

## Investment Committee minutes

1. AL sends memo to CEO.
2. AL answers in witten CEO questions/comments.
3. CEO selects up to 2 participants for the memo review meeting in addition to the peer reviewers.
4. AL schedules memo review meeting.

---

# Legal due diligence

## Completed legal due diligence.

1. Once the memo is approved, the AL informs the AD a legal due diligence will start.
2. AD will indicate the AL the documents and form the external organzatiom has to submit.
3. AL will send to the external organization the list of documents, form and support materials to ease the fill out.
4. Orgs complete the form and submit documents.
5. AD validates with the trust.

(If additional revisions are necessary, the flow restarts.)

---

# Onboarding

## Kick-off minutes

1. AL schedule kick off meeting
2. AL, AT and organization review the KPIs, timing and scope.
3. AL, AT and organization review reporting conditions and options of support (MEL, Tech, strategic)


#### *ACRONYMS*
- CEO: Chief Executive Office
- AL: Area lead, it could be VP and directors depending on the area
- AD: Administrative director
- TL: Tech lead
- KMD: Knowledge management director
- AT: Area team
- ORG: refers to external organization or initiative. In the case of venture building this is replaced by the AL.
